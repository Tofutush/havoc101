Triggered by: Completing [[Find the thief]]

## Description

You're pretty sure you got the right person. Now all you have to do is confront them and get everyone's stolen stuff back! Remember to bring a weapon and save your game. Completing this mission may lead to permanent endings.

## How to complete

### If the thief is correct

- [[Piano hall]]
- Achieve [[Dilettante Detective]]
- If [[player]] has the [[gun]] as her weapon
	- Shoot the thief and achieve [[Vicious Vigilante]]
	- Don't shoot the thief and achieve [[Paper Tiger]]
- If [[player]]'s weapon *isn't* the gun
	- If the gun is still in the [[piano seat]], the thief shoots the player, and the game ends in [[Bloodbath]]
	- If the gun is not in the piano seat, the thief locks the player inside the seat, and the game ends in [[Outmatched]]

### If the thief is incorrect

- [[cafeteria]]
- Achieve [[Outsmarted]]
