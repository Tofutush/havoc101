## Description

Set the school on fire.

## How to get

- Get [[oil]] from the [[cafeteria]]
	- If you want to go into the [[kitchen]], the chefs will stop you
	- Watch cutscene of someone dropping their water bottle in the cafeteria and being told to go into the kitchen to get it
	- Drop your [[water bottle]]
	- Get out and get back in
	- Go into the kitchen and get oil
- Get a [[lighter]]
- Pour oil somewhere and light it
