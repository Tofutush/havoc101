Triggered by: Completing [[Find the thief]]

## Description

You're pretty sure you got the right person. Now all you have to do is confront them and get everyone's stolen stuff back! Remember to bring a weapon and save your game. Completing this mission may lead to permanent endings.

## How to complete

- If the thief is correct
	- [[Piano hall]]
		- If [[player]] has the [[gun]] as her weapon
			- Shoot the thief
				- Achieve [[Vicious Vigilante]]
			- Don't shoot the thief
				- Achieve [[Paper Tiger]]
		- If [[player]]'s weapon isn't gun
			- If the gun is still in the piano seat
				- The thief shoots the player
				- Ending [[Bloodbath]]
			- If the gun is not in the piano seat
				- The thief locks the player inside the seat
				- Ending [[Outmatched]]
	- Achieve [[Dilettante Detective]]
- If the thief is incorrect
	- [[cafeteria]]
	- Achieve [[Outsmarted]]
