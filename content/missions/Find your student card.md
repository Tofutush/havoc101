The first mission.

## Description

Without your student card, you can't open your locker, go to the library, or eat in the cafeteria! You have to find it. Why not look in your classroom?

## How to complete

- go to classroom ([[D502]])
- find note in desk drawer
	- "Hey, you left your card in your drawer last weekend. I put it in my locker. I'm probably in the cafeteria right now, come find me. -- Sparky."
- Triggers [[Go to the cafeteria and find Sparky]]
- Follow [[Sparky]] to her locker
- Get card
- Triggers [[Get armed]]
